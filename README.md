# E-Commerce-Insights-
E-Commerce &amp; Marketing Insights dashboard built in Power BI Desktop. Implements custom DAX measures for AOV, ROAS, and conversion metrics, an optimized 3-section layout grid, dual performance-control slicer buttons, and a clean corporate visual theme. Include full dataset schema and README.

The dashboard is structured into three clean, easy-to-scan rows:

1.The Quick Summary (Top): Big, clear cards tracking the numbers that matter most—Total Revenue, Average Order Value, and ROAS (Return on Ad Spend, or how much cash we make for every dollar spent on ads).
2.The Trend Tracker (Middle): A combined chart that layers our monthly revenue right next to our monthly marketing budget. It instantly lets you spot if a sales spike was worth the advertising cost.
3.The Deep Dives (Bottom): Breakdown charts that show our 5 product categories (Electronics, Clothing, Books, Home Appliances, and Toys) and our 3 main sales regions (North America, Europe, and Asia).
